The Difference

Vendors

We manage so many properties that we get volume discounts from vendors and we are able to pass those savings on to our clients. This includes everything from painting contractors, to plumbers, to landscapers. We have pre-negotiated prices with all of our vendors. This alone may save our clients hundreds, even thousands of dollars annually.

Low Tenant Turnover

Nearly 70 percent of our tenants renew their leases. Our proven system of finding and keeping quality tenants keeps that percentage high.

Strategic Price Increases

We increase rents by $30 a year and have a 75 percent success rate with that.

Latest Technology

We utilize the latest online technology to manage our properties. Everything, from rent payments, to work orders can be done through our owner and renter portals. Renters and owners alike love our user friendly website.
